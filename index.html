Agenda

Agenda is a browser based standalone tool to track the time table of meetings.


In Agenda you can configure a list of meetings with all of the agenda entries. When a meeting is started the current agenda entry is highlighted. The rest of the time is shown and the time when the meeting will end is shown, too.


This app is a PWA. Thus you can view it in the online browser. Just visit Agenda at my homepage.


You can use the app on your PC and on your mobile device.


If you want to use the app without an internet connection, you can store it for offline usage. This is depending ony your browser. A detailed guide is provided here.
